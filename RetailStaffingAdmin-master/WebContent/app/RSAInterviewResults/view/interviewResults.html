User Information
Select A Requisition
Only active requisitions are displayed. If no requisitions are displayed, there are no active requisitions for your location.
Select A Candidate
Only candidates being actively considered for an active requisition will be displayed. If you have selected an active requisition and no candidates are displayed, there are no active candidates attached to the requisition.
Interview Details
Once interview details have been entered and saved, they cannot be changed.
*If the candidate is present, you may move forward to the Offer Details, Candidate Personal Data, and the Background Check sections before completing the SIG details and results. The SIG Interview Details must be completed before submitting the Interview Summary Form.
Select a value for each structured interview question using the answer scale below.
If a question number was not asked, select 'N/A'. A value is required for all 10 questions (this includes 'N/A').
5 = Highly Favorable, 4 = Favorable, 3 = Acceptable, 2 = Unfavorable, 1 = Highly Unfavorable, N/A = Not Applicable
Based on the Interview Results entered, the overall interview result is unfavorable. Candidates who receive an overall result of unfavorable cannot be made an offer.
